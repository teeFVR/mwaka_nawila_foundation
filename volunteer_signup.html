Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need specific qualifications to volunteer? expand_more

While some roles (like medical support) require certification, many of our youth empowerment and community roles only require passion and a commitment to learn.

What is the minimum time commitment? expand_more

We suggest at least 4 hours per week for 3 months to see real impact, but we have one-off event opportunities as well.

Will I receive training? expand_more

Yes! Every volunteer undergoes an orientation session and specific training for their chosen focus area.
