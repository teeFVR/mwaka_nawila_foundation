Our Mission

Driven by passion, sustained by community commitment.

The Mwaka Nawila Foundation is a Zambian-based NGO committed to fostering hope and providing essential resources to the youth. We believe that by focusing on health, education, and social protection, we can unlock the potential of the next generation.
