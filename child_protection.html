Our Mission for Children

At Mwaka Nawila Foundation, our mission is to create a shield of protection around every child, ensuring they grow up in an environment free from abuse, neglect, and exploitation.

Reporting & Support

If you suspect a child is in danger or needs assistance, please contact our emergency response team immediately. All reports are handled with absolute confidentiality.

call

24/7 Hotline

+1 (800) SAFE-KIDS

mail

Confidential Email

report@mwakanawila.org

What to expect:

  1. 1

    Immediate initial assessment by a social worker

  2. 2

    Coordination with local authorities if necessary

  3. 3

    Placement in safe care within 2-4 hours
