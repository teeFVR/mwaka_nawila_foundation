Our Mission

To provide sustainable resources, quality education, and innovative economic opportunities to underserved communities, ensuring long-term self-reliance and growth.

visibility

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of their background, has the access and agency to lead a life of dignity, health, and limitless potential.

Our History

Founded in 2015, Mwaka Nawila Foundation began as a small community-led initiative in response to the growing educational gap in rural districts. What started with a single classroom and three volunteers has blossomed into a multi-national effort.

Our journey has been defined by the stories of those we serve. From drilling our first well to launching the Women’s Vocational Program, every milestone is a testament to the power of community collaboration and transparent leadership.
