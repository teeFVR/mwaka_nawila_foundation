Our Flagship Program

Empowering the Girl Child through Education

Breaking systemic barriers and creating a brighter future for every girl in rural Zambia through quality schooling.

"The journey to school is often more than just a walk; it's a battle against poverty and tradition."

The Context

The Challenge in Zambia

In many Zambian communities, girls face overwhelming barriers to education. Financial hardships prioritize boys' schooling, while long, unsafe distances to institutions deter families from sending daughters.

  • error_outline

    Economic Barriers

    High costs of uniforms, books, and tuition fees often force girls out of the classroom.

  • distance

    Distance & Safety

    Daily walks exceeding 10km put girls at risk of gender-based violence and exhaustion.

  • group

    Cultural Expectations

    Early marriage and household responsibilities frequently truncate academic journeys.
