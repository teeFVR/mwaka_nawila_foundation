Chipo's Journey to Tech

After losing both parents, Chipo struggled to stay in school. Through our 'Girls in Tech' initiative, she received a full scholarship and a laptop. Today, she is a junior developer at a leading fintech firm in Lusaka.

Mainza's Sustainable Farm

Mainza participated in our 'Youth Agritech' workshop in Choma. We provided him with initial seeds and sustainable irrigation equipment. He now manages a farm that employs 15 other youths from his community.
